The All-in-One Command Center for Power Users
The Skyrim SE/AE Control Center Engine is a portable, lightweight terminal-based utility built to centralize your entire modding workflow. Designed for power users, it replaces the need to hunt through Windows Explorer, browser bookmarks, or multiple taskbars. From launching your mod manager to deep-level plugin auditing, every aspect of your load order management is now unified in one high-performance console. 

Setup & Configuration: Upon your first launch, the engine creates a config.txt file to store your paths. This file is fully editable, allowing you to update your setup on the fly. If you ever need to reset your configuration, you can simply delete the file or use the built-in reset feature in the main menu.

Core Capabilities:

Workflow Centralization: Effortlessly jump to the Nexus Mods hub, launch Vortex in the background, or boot your game (SKSE or Vanilla). The tool automatically terminates upon game launch to ensure zero background resource usage while you play.

Pro-Mod Diagnostic Hub: An integrated, expert-level toolkit for monitoring engine limits (ESP/ESM caps), identifying heavy assets, auditing missing master dependencies, and performing automated save-file maintenance.

Crash Log & Directory Management: A dedicated explorer to keep your data files and crash logs organized and instantly accessible.

Portable & Non-Intrusive: The engine lives in your environment, requiring no installation. It interacts with your game and mod manager settings safely without making permanent modifications to your system.

Why this tool is the perfect companion for your Mod Manager
While Vortex or MO2 excel at the "heavy lifting" of installation and sorting, they are primarily user-facing interfaces. The Pro-Mod Diagnostic Hub works at the system level. It is designed to act as your "Pre-Flight Checklist," verifying the physical integrity of your load order and storage health outside of your manager's virtual environment. It does not replace your manager; instead, it provides a "clean room" view of your installation that standard managers were not built to display. While the Control Center Engine manages your daily workflow (1-5, 7), the Diagnostic Hub (6) ensures your technical foundation remains stable.

Who is this for? This tool is crafted for anyone—from seasoned veterans managing 300+ plugins to newcomers needing a cleaner, professional way to interact with their game. Whether you are troubleshooting a crash or keeping your load order pristine, this Engine provides the data you need in seconds
